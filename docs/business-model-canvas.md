# Business Model Canvas

## Project Title
RSU Nexus – Campus Resource Marketplace

## 1. Customer Segments
- Primary users: RSU students who need to borrow, lend, request, or offer academic equipment, learning resources, and student services.
- Secondary users: Student clubs and authorized administrators who contribute resources or moderate marketplace activities.

## 2. Value Proposition
RSU Nexus provides a trusted and organized campus marketplace where students can discover equipment, learning resources, and student services; submit requests; and keep track of availability or request status. It improves access, reduces unnecessary purchases, and makes peer support easier to find.

## 3. Channels
- Web application accessible through desktop and mobile browsers.
- University announcements and student orientation.
- Social media and student community groups.
- QR codes shared around the campus.

## 4. Customer Relationships
- User registration and login.
- User profiles and listing management.
- Confirmation and status feedback for listings and requests.
- Private request/contact flow between requesters and providers.
- Ratings and reviews may be considered after the first MVP is validated.

## 5. Revenue Streams / Sustainability Logic
The prototype is not intended to generate direct revenue. Its value comes from:
- Supporting sustainable reuse of campus resources.
- Reducing unnecessary purchasing costs for students.
- Improving campus resource sharing efficiency.
- Future opportunities may include promoted listings or university sponsorship.

## 6. Key Resources
- Development team members.
- HTML5, CSS3, and vanilla JavaScript.
- Supabase Authentication, PostgreSQL, and Storage.
- Vercel hosting.
- GitHub repository and collaboration workflow.
- Figma design files and approved project assets.

## 7. Key Activities
- Develop and maintain the web application.
- Manage user accounts and marketplace listings.
- Provide search and filtering functions.
- Monitor and moderate inappropriate listings.
- Maintain database and system performance.

## 8. Key Partners
- RSU student community and student organizations.
- Rangsit University only if formal approval is obtained for official deployment or branding.
- Project supervisor/lecturer.
- Web hosting provider.
- GitHub for version control.

## 9. Cost Structure
- Development time.
- Internet and hosting services.
- Domain (if deployed publicly).
- Maintenance and bug fixes.
- Design and collaboration tools.

## Final Note
This business model directly supports the MVP features of RSU Nexus, including verified access, listing creation, marketplace browsing, search and filtering, detail views, a request/contact workflow, status tracking, My Listings management, admin moderation, and a dashboard summary. Full real-time chat, payments, complex booking, ratings, and reviews remain outside the first MVP scope.
